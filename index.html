About CHM college

Starting as an institution to cater to the aspirations of the Minority Sindhi Community, Smt. CHM College, with its forward-looking approach, good infrastructure, committed faculty and enthusiastic student community belonging to every class of society, stands as a classic example of diverse learning today. Smt. CHM College is proud of its glorious history which defines a tradition of high value. The College is the brainchild of some of the most enlightened educationists of our times, including Principal K. M. Kundnani and Barrister H.G. Advani and Mr. Gangaram Mansukhani. After being displaced from his native land Sindh, now in Pakistan because of partition of India on 15th August 1947, Principal K. M. Kundnani reached Mumbai with a mission to resurrect National College which he had to close down at Sindh. No doubt, he lost his native place but established an empire bubbling with a host of educational institutions in Mumbai. He initiated the establishment of fourteen educational institutions with a view to arousing intellectual and spiritual strength of the people. And he had to work with girt towards the fulfillment of that dream and to keep on expanding the frontiers of that dream so that it encompasses an educational empire that goes by the name of the HSNC Board today.
